AISYWC

IEEE and its members inspire a global community through IEEE’s highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.AISYWC is one of the many efforts made by IEEE to bring the community a bit closer.

The All India Student – Young Professionals – Women in Engineering Congress is the annual hallmark event of the IEEE India Council. Founded in 2000, the AIS(YW)C has grown over the years to become the largest event on the calendar for IEEE members in India. It is a conglomeration of professionals, entrepreneurs, visionaries and some of the greatest minds in the country. By bringing together amazing people from all walks of life, the AISYWC empowers its participants to be inspired for more, collaborate together and innovate for tomorrow. Every year, AIS(YW)C is held at a different venue giving students, from all over the country, a chance to gain the maximum benefit from it.

In 2011, the proper organization of the congress started with IEEE Delhi Section. AISC’11 was held at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi from 29 September to 1st October, 2011. In the following years, the congress has been organized successfully in many other sections including Gujarat, Bangalore, Madras. In 2015 AISC evolved to be known as AISYWC ,hosted by Kerala Section and by IEEE Delhi Section along with IEEE Rajasthan Subsection at LNM Information Institute of Technology in Jaipur, Rajasthan from 7th-9th October, 2016.

IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, Incorporated (IEEE) is a non-profit corporation, incorporated in the state of New York on 16 March 1896. The purpose of the IEEE is to engage exclusively in scientific and educational activities directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of electrical, electronics, communications and computer engineering, as well as computer science, the allied branches of engineering and the related arts and sciences, which benefit the engineering profession and the public making it the world’s largest professional body working towards advancement of technology for humanity.
Official Website : www.ieee.org

IEEE INDIA COUNCIL

IEEE India Council ,currently chaired by Sivaji Chakravorti, is the umbrella organisation which coordinates IEEE activities in India. Its primary aim is to assist and coordinate the activities of local “Sections”, in order to benefit mutually, and avoid duplication of effort and resources. IEEE India Council was established on 20th May 1976 and is one of the five councils in the Asia Pacific Region (Region #10 of IEEE).
Know more : http://www.ewh.ieee.org/r10/india_council/indiacouncil.html

IEEE UP SECTION

IEEE UP Section is one of the most active sections of IEEE India Council. It is the controlling body of all types of IEEE related activities in the state of Uttar Pradesh in India. The Uttar Pradesh Section is located in Region 10, and is represented at the India Council. The Section was formed on 11 May 1992. It has been continuously increasing for last three years in terms of opening new student branches, new student chapters, section chapters and recently Nepal subsection.
Know More : http://www.ieeeup.org/
