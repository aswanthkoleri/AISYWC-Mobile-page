AISYWC'17

2017 is going to be the another successful instance of the event started by IEEE India Council years back. This year the Congress comes with the theme “Imagine, Engineer, Empower and Enlighten” throwing light on the fact that in the last 10 years of human-advancement, the technology has evolved more than been innovated. None has created a flying car yet, but the cars on road have evolved to a better version of itself. No unbreakable cellphones have been devised, but the already existing ones are becoming more and more robust and secure everyday.
AISYWC’17 will be the source through we will be enlightened about the vast ocean of opportunities and possibilities we have open in front of us, which can be utilised to improve the existing technology in use

VENUE

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad (IIIT-Allahabad) is a public university located in Allahabad, in Uttar Pradesh,India. It is one of the five Indian Institutes of Information Technology funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development. The Institution was founded in 1999, and in the following year received university status and the right to award its own degrees. In 2014 the IIIT Act was passed, under which IIITA and four other Institutes of Information Technology funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development were classed as Institutes of National Importance.
The Institute is known for its excellence in fields of IT education, with the best of infrastructure and atmosphere that helps the students to grow and learn.

Young Professionals

The IEEE Young Professionals is an international community of enthusiastic, dynamic and innovative members and volunteers from across the globe.Members of this community aim at elevating their professional image, expanding their global network, connecting with peers locally and simultaneously making a contribution to the community through their knowledge, passion and determination.The formation of YP Affinity Group of UP Section was announced in May 2016.

The IEEE YP R10 co-ordinator is Mr. Nivas Ravichandran, currently taking care of over 35 YP Affinity Groups in Asia-Pacific region. Mr. Gitansh Anand (Delhi Section) acts as the Vice Chairperson of YP India Council

The Student Activities Committee

The Student Activities Committee(SAC) acts as the network between the various student branches/sections and associated student members. SAC provides a single platform for students from all over the region to interact,innovate and exchange ideas in numerous activities organized and executed for the benefit of students. SAC enables students to enhance their technical and leadership skills through seminars, conferences and competitions.

Rajesh Ingle is the current R10 Student Activities coordinator.Mr. Gitansh Anand is the Student Activities Coordinator for IEEE India Council while Dr. J. Ramkumar of UP Section acts as Vice Chairperson.

Women in Engineering

The IEEE Women in Engineering is one of the largest organizationsin the world dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls globally to follow their academic interests through a career in engineering.WIE strives to increase and promote recruitment of women in technical arena and recognize their outstanding contributions to the community. WIE also aims at developing such activities that would inspire girls at the very primary level of education to take up a technical profession in future. Every year more number of Affinity Groups are formed to continue with the aim of WIE in every part of the world. The first WIE Affinity Group in UP Section was officially formed in 2014. Currently WIE-AG UP consists of over 373 members.
Jing Dong of IEEE Beijing Section succeeded Celia Shahnaz as IEEE WIE R10 co-ordinator
Dr.Rajshree Jain of Maharashtra Section is the current Vice Chairperson of WIE-India Council.
