VENUE

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad

IIIT-Allahabad is a public university located in Allahabad, in Uttar Pradesh,India. It is one of the five Indian Institutes of Information Technology funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development. The Institution was founded in 1999, and in the following year received university status and the right to award its own degrees. In 2014 the IIIT Act was passed, under which IIITA and four other Institutes of Information Technology funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development were classed as Institutes of National Importance.
The Institute is known for its excellence in fields of IT education, with the best of infrastructure and atmosphere that helps the students to grow and learn. IIITA has grown quickly in a very short time and will continue on the path in the coming years. Among engineering colleges in India, IIIT-Allahabad ranked 11th by India Today in 2017 and 12 by Outlook India in 2017.

IEEE Student Branch
IIIT-Aallahabad

IEEE Student Branch IIITA is a team of enthusiastic students, research scholars and faculty members of IIIT-Allahabad, that join together to form a young team that links the Institute to the world's largest professional society.
IEEE SB IIITA has been organizing events and conferences as such to mould their students into better members of the community.
Recently this year, IIITA hosted IEEE Computer Society India Symposium 2017 from March 17 to March 19, themed “Humans and Computers”. Attended by eminent personalities including Mrs. Moumita Dutta (ISRO) ,Davesh Singh(Assistant Director, UIDAI) and Mr Santosh Sharma (Author ,”Dissolve the Box Thinking”) the event was a huge success and efforts of students were highly appreciated by all.
